    • A multilingual fork of Kreate, built with performance improvements, UI/UX refinement, and new features.



    Modern & Intuitive UI.

    Enjoy a refined user experience with a beautiful interface, seamless navigation, and YouTube integration.

    Advanced Lyrics Support.

    Fetch, display, edit, synchronize, and translate lyrics in real-time.

    Immersive Audio Visualizer.

    Enjoy real-time visual effects synchronized perfectly with your music.

    Discord Rich Presence.

    Show off your current musical vibe by displaying your playing track directly on your Discord profile.

    Theme Presets.

    Choose from 6 beautifully crafted visual themes to personalize your music experience.

    YouTube Integration.

    Recommendations and profile-related content are synchronized directly from your YouTube account.

    Listening Statistics.

    Track your listening habits, favorite artists, and playback trends over time.

    Smart Offline Caching.

    Automatically cache songs for offline playback with customizable limits, or download tracks for permanent access.

    Search & Voice Search.

    Easily find your favorite tracks, albums, and artists, even with your voice.

    OTA Updates.

    Receive automatic in-app updates directly from GitHub without needing to manually download new APKs.

    Carousel Player View.

    Enjoy a unique, visually stunning 3D carousel view for your music playback.

    Detailed Artist Profiles.

    Explore comprehensive artist profiles with top tracks, albums, and similar artists.

    Advanced Audio Controls.

    Customize your experience with advanced audio controls, backup/restore features, and more.

    Playlists Management.

    Easily create, import, export, and manage your playlists in one unified interface.

    Playback Queue.

    Keep track of what's playing next, rearrange songs on the fly, and manage your upcoming tracks.

    Complete History.

    Never lose a song you loved. Access your complete listening history at any time.

    Multiple Device Compatibility.

    Enjoy your music everywhere! N-Zik is compatible with Android Mobile, Android Auto, Android Automotive, and Android TV. (Later Wear OS).
